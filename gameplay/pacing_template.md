# Pacing Template Library v1

## Safe_Start
Low-pressure onboarding or staging window where the player can orient, place squad members, and read objectives.

## Escalation
Threat density or decision pressure increases. Introduce crossfire, detection risk, or time pressure.

## Intensity_Spike
Short high-risk window requiring decisive action: TACTICAL BREACH, rescue, defense peak, or counterattack.

## Short_Recovery
Brief stabilization phase for reload, reposition, wounded management, or objective reassessment.

## Climax_Final_Push
Final commitment phase that resolves the mission with the clearest tactical and narrative payoff.

## Usage rule
Every mission/feature should explicitly state which pacing template it enters, extends, or transitions into.
